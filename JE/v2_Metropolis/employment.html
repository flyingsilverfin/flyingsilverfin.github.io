Stage Manager (£70)

Full time

Stage Managers supervise various forms of entertainment, ensuring that transition between acts and technical equipment is running smoothly. Similar to Area Supervisor, this is a position of particular responsibility and technical experience is highly desirable.

Area Supervisor(£65)

Full time

This is a highly responsible role. Area supervisors are assigned to a particular group of stalls and must ensure that other workers are performing their tasks correctly and that the event is running smoothly in their area. Less labour intensive than other jobs, this role requires excellent communication and leadership skills. We would highly recommend that potential Area Supervisors outline previous relevant experience in their applications.

FOOD & DRINK (£61 / under 21yrs: £48)

Full time

Food & Drink workers staff different stalls at the event. This may involve the preparation of food and drink or serving it directly to guests. Throughout the event, food & drink workers will liaise with others on the need to replenish supplies. Communication skills and general competence are important for this role.

RECYCLING (£61)

Full time

In recycling, workers ensure that waste is removed rapidly and that the event is consistently clean. Recycling workers will spend a lot of time roaming around different areas of the event. Those applying for this role should be efficient and dedicated.

SECURITY (£61; under 21yrs: £48)

Full time

Security workers ensure that nobody enters the event without a ticket. This role requires adaptability and teamwork skills. The muscle of the operation.

Half on/Half off

There are a limited number of half on/half off positions, priority for which goes to Trinity Hall students. Roles include showing workers and acts to relevant areas, managing stalls, working in queues, managing Photo Booth, and talking to/directing people. Working half the event entitles you to the other half free!
